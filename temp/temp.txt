### description

Data Saturday Durban is a free, full-day, volunteer-led, in-person event that covers a variety of topics within the Microsoft data platform ecosystem. The event will take place in the Uni4 campus in Westville, Durban, South Africa.

Formally known as SQL Saturday Durban, this event provides attendees the opportunity to interact with their fellow data professionals to network and discuss challenges and solutions to some of their most pressing problems.
