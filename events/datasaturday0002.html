Data Saturday #2 Guatemala
Jan 23 2021 - Virtual

Data Saturday Guatemala es un evento virtual de capacitación en línea para profesionales que utilizan la plataforma de datos de Microsoft.

Data Saturday Guatemala is a virtual online training event for professionals who use the Microsoft data platform.

Community speakers will offer sessions about cloud, analytics, database administration, business intelligence, AI and more.

Due to the COVID pandemic, this event will be held online.
